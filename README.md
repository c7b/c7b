### 🚀 What I Do

I work at the intersection of **engineering and e-commerce operations**. My focus isn't "building websites" anymore, it's building systems: checkout surfaces, automation, localization, internal dashboards, and tooling that catches operational issues before they get expensive.

#### 🏢 Current Focus
Senior engineer on global **Shopify Plus** infrastructure for a multi-region D2C operation.
* **Systems:** Checkout extensibility, internal tooling, and revenue-protection automation.
* **Reliability:** Building diagnostic scripts that monitor storefront health, product data, and pricing across markets.
* **Scope:** Localization, post-purchase flows, and the operational layer between marketing, ops, and engineering.
